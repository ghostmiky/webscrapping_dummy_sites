Animal tissues

The animal body is also made up of different types of cells. Example :- The human body is made up of about 210 different types of cells.

There are groups of cells with common origin to perform a particular function in the multicellular animal body.

Main types of animal tissues are given below.

  • Epithelial tissue

  • Connective tissue

  • Muscle tissue

  • Nervous tissue
