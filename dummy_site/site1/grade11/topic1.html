Plant tissue

You may have observed that plant tissues are of different forms. Animal tissues are also of different forms. It is observable that different cell types are present in living beings and similar cells are arranged together. A group of cells with a common origin that has been modified to perform particular functions in the body is known as a tissue.

  • Classification of plant tissues

We can identify the nature of a growing root from the above observation. Growing part is soft and light coloured. Mature part is rough and dark coloured. This is because of the nature of tissues.

Plant tissues can be categorised using different criteria. Plant tissues can be divided into two groups according to the ability of cell division.

  • Meristematic tissues

  • Permanent tissues
